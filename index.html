Ng-Nistx

Ng-Nistx is an AngularJS custom component NPM library and SCSS classes as a wrapper around PrimeNG. With 2 fully custom components, and the rest as SCSS styling to customize PrimeNG, this library speeds up development, creates a consistant look and feel, and inspires client confidence that they will recive their product faster and cheaper.

I pitched the idea of Ng-Nistx to the ceo and fellow co-workers after meetings about 5 upcomming projects. I took full charge of creating the entire library and ensuring that all its code is easy to maintain and each component is reuasable in any project. I designed it to be very easy to customize and wrote highly detailed documentation to ensure all future devs can understand how to use the library.

Aissessor

Aissessor is an investment risk assesment platform built for Schneider Downs that uses AI to automatically give a risk rating to each investment. Schnieder Downs assesors take up a client and fill out a risk assesment form, upload relevant documents, and the AI give a risk assesment on the investment as well as each section on the form.

I developed the entire Front-End using AngularJS and PrimeNG. Being the sole Front-End developer on this project, I was able to make several UI and UX changes from the original XD mocukups that vastly improved User Experience. A few things I improved are how assesments are deleted and color scheme UI upgrades to the assesment forms for easier reading and showing the difference betweeen scores.

AngularJS TypeScript SCSS HTML5 PrimeNG Bootstrap 4 MongoDB

DQE Calulator

Wanting custom designed AI for your business to help reduce costs, product loss, plan deliveries, or anything else and worried how much it may cost? Look no further with Cognistx's DQE Calculator. With this you can estimate what your cost may be for your custom designed solution.

As a fully solo project with the company, I was given full control over how the project would be built. Following XD mocukups, I choose to build the calculator in react for easy state management. While I mainly focused on the Front-End end, I directed the team to use ExpressJS for the Back-End APIs.

MedTracker

Do you need accountability when you take your medicine? Do you need to track when you take your medicine? MedTracker offers a great and easy way track your medicine and keep you accountable. Just enter your medicine, set reminder notifications, log when you take it, and the app will send a text message to 2 people of your choice.

I designed the app in React-Native for easy porting to android phones as my main target. I built the back-end in ExpressJS for my APIs and MySQL as my database. I use AWS SNS, API Gateway, and Lambda to handle sending SMS text messages to users when they take their medicine.
